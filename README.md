# Rate Radar

Compare USD to INR conversion rates across multiple platforms and banks in real time. See exactly what you'll receive after all fees, markups, and taxes — so you can pick the option that puts the most rupees in your account.

---

## What It Does

If you receive USD payments in India (as a freelancer, consultant, or business), every platform charges differently — some take a percentage, some mark up the exchange rate, some add service charges and GST. Comparing them manually is tedious and error-prone.

This tool does the math for you. Enter a USD amount and instantly see a side-by-side breakdown of your final INR amount across all supported platforms, ranked from best to worst.

## Supported Platforms

| Platform | Type |
|---|---|
| Skydo | Fintech |
| Mulya | Fintech |
| Infinity App | Fintech |
| IDFC First Bank | Bank |
| Indian Overseas Bank (IOB) | Bank |

## Features

- **Live rates** — Exchange rates are fetched in real time from each platform
- **True cost comparison** — Shows the effective rate you receive after all deductions, not just the quoted forex rate
- **Best / worst highlighting** — The best and worst options are visually highlighted
- **Amount-sensitive** — Fee structures vary by transfer size; results update when you change the amount
- **Responsive** — Works on desktop and mobile
